# Example Platform — Personas

> Neutral fixture personas for the service-blueprint example. The persona
> **type** is what classifies front/back-stage in the rendered blueprint.

---

### P-01 · Requesting User — releases slots they cannot use

**Persona type:** Customer
**Tier:** 1
**Lifecycle status:** Active

> *"If I can't make it, freeing my slot should take seconds — and someone else should get it."*

---

### P-02 · Coordinator — validates releases and rebookings

**Persona type:** Primary
**Tier:** 1
**Lifecycle status:** Active

> *"I sit between the released slot and the next person who needs it."*

---

### P-03 · Operations Analyst — reconciles and audits freed capacity

**Persona type:** Secondary
**Tier:** 2
**Lifecycle status:** Active

> *"I never talk to a user, but I make sure no freed slot is ever lost."*
